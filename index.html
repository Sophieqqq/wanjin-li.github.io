02. About Me

Hello I'm Wanjin Li, My wide-ranging knowledge on computer science and multimedia art skills enabled me to incorporate my aesthetics into my programming endeavors and created vivid applications, such as a computer game based on Unity 3D.

I noticed that human factor should be the core of system design to match the objectives of its function and to analyze the data retrieved from experiments. After confirming that the system needs to be highly interactive and be able to respond to human behaviors, research is necessary to determine what behavioral data offer insight into the processes, and designers should design feedback to tailor the situation. In the process of conducting research and consulting references on cognitive science and system design, I developed profound awareness that further research in such a multidisciplinary area requires systematic analytical skills and knowledge in diverse fields.
